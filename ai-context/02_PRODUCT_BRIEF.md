# Product Brief — PRODEPT 2026

## What Is It?

An internal DEPT prediction app for the FIFA World Cup 2026. Employees predict match scores, earn points based on accuracy, and compete on leaderboards — individually and in private leagues.

## Who Is It For?

DEPT employees worldwide who want to engage with the World Cup in a fun, competitive, social way within their company.

## Jobs to Be Done

1. Register and log in with email and password
2. See upcoming matches and make score predictions
3. View World Cup groups and standings
4. Check my points, global rank, and prediction history
5. Create or join private leagues with friends and colleagues
6. Compete on leaderboards and feel the matchday energy
7. Feel like I'm part of something social and fun — not filling out a form

## Current MVP (Built)

- Landing page, login, and registration
- Home dashboard: global rank, total points, predictions count, private leagues count, next match with prediction preview
- Predictions: full match list, per-match prediction form with score pickers, lock before kickoff
- Groups: World Cup group viewer with all 12 groups and 48 teams
- Leagues: create leagues with custom or auto-generated invite codes, join via code, per-league leaderboards with member rankings
- Global league: "PRODEPT Main League" shared by all users
- Match cards with team flags, dates, stages, prediction display
- Bottom navigation (Home, Groups, Predictions, Leagues)

## Future Improvements

- Admin panel (enter match results, trigger points recalculation, manage users)
- Standalone global leaderboard page
- User profile page with stats and prediction history
- Badges, streaks, and achievements
- Push notifications before matches
- Match status management (SCHEDULED -> LIVE -> FINISHED transitions)
- Stats and analytics (best predictors, most popular scores, etc.)
